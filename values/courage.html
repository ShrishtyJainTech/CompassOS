🦁 Courage

"Courage doesn’t always roar. Sometimes courage is the quiet voice at the end of the day saying, ‘I will try again tomorrow.’" — Mary Anne Radmacher

Why Courage Matters to Me

To grow is to be uncomfortable. Courage gives me the strength to try, to fall, and to keep showing up even when the outcome is uncertain. It’s my promise to myself that fear won’t define my choices.

My Commitments

  • Say “yes” to things that scare me (and “no” to things that drain me)
  • Own my truth, even when my voice shakes
  • Celebrate brave moments—big *and* small
