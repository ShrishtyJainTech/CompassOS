🌸 Kindness

"Do things for people not because of who they are or what they do in return, but because of who you are." — Harold S. Kushner

Why Kindness Matters to Me

Kindness is my anchor. It means showing up with gentleness in a world that often forgets to pause. I believe in the ripple effect of small acts—how a word or a smile at the right time can quietly change someone’s day.

My Commitments

  • Offer one intentional act of kindness daily (even a silent one)
  • Practice kindness toward myself, especially when I fail
  • Recognize kindness in others and reflect it back
